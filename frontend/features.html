Platform Capabilities

A comprehensive suite of tools designed to modernize urban waste management end-to-end.

📱

Citizen Reporting System

Allow residents to act as eyes on the street. With our mobile-ready interface, citizens can submit localized reports of illegal dumping or missed collections complete with photo evidence and GPS coordinates.

🛰️

Real-Time Waste Tracking

IOT-enabled dumpsters and bins ping our servers with fill levels. The system dynamically monitors city-wide waste generation to anticipate needs before bins overflow.

💻

Smart Dashboard

City administrators gain access to a centralized command center. View live truck locations, resolve pending reports, and analyze historic trends to make data-driven decisions.

🗺️

Driver Route Optimization

Stop sending trucks to empty bins. Our AI algorithm calculates the most efficient daily collection routes based on live traffic, bin capacity, and pending priority reports.

🎁

Eco-Points Reward System

Incentivize positive behavior. Citizens earn Eco-Points for verified reports, recycling efforts, and reducing waste. Points can be redeemed for local municipal perks or discounts at partnered businesses.
