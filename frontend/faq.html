Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about navigating the SmartWaste platform and its features.

❓ How do I report a missed waste collection?

Navigate to the Citizen Portal or download our mobile application. Click on "Report Issue", take a photo of the location, describe the problem, and our system will automatically stamp the report with GPS coordinates.

❓ How are Eco-Points calculated?

Eco-Points are awarded based on positive environmental actions. You earn 10 points for a verified dumping report, 50 points for registering as a recycling advocate, and bonus points whenever a driver confirms an area you reported has been cleaned.

❓ How long does collection normally take after a report?

Priority issues (like biohazards or blocked roads) are dispatched immediately. Standard missed collection reports are automatically added to a driver's optimized route and are typically resolved within 24-48 hours.

❓ I am an Admin. How do I access the dashboard?

City officials can log in via the "Dashboard" link in the navigation menu. Ensure you have authorized credentials provided by your municipality's IT department. Use the login button at the top right for instant access.
